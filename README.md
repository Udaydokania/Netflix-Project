## Project Overview
This project performs an in-depth **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Netflix dataset to understand content trends, genre distribution, and audience engagement. By leveraging **Python**, I've extracted insights regarding which genres dominate the platform and how filming trends have evolved over time.

## Key Insights & Conclusions
The analysis successfully answered five core questions regarding the Netflix library:

* **Q1: Most Frequent Genre** **Drama** is the most frequent genre, appearing more than **14%** of the time among 20 different genre categories.
* **Q2: Highest Audience Engagement** Popularity follows frequency; **Drama** holds the highest fan popularity, representing over **18.5%** of highly-voted movies.
* **Q3: Most Popular Title** **Spider-Man: No Way Home** achieved the highest popularity rate, spanning the Action, Adventure, and Science Fiction genres.
* **Q4: Lowest Popularity Title** The film **"The United States, Thread"** recorded the lowest popularity rate in this specific dataset.
* **Q5: Peak Production Year** **2020** was identified as the year with the highest filming rate, marking a significant peak in content volume.
